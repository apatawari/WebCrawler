Unlike the newer Blurs, this was made right here in the USA, and because it's an '04, it has both post mounts AND disc mounts. What's more, the extra $300 that was paid for the anodized version of the frame means that it's extremely scratch resistant.

It's set up with an Avid Speed Dial 7 /Avid Arch Rival brake V-brake system, which makes it a whole lot lighter than with hydraulics, and IMHO, a whole lot easier to stop than with mechanical disc calipers.
